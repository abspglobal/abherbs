Straight forward scenario:

  1. Choose color of the flower by clicking on image.
  2. Choose habitat by clicking on image.
  3. Choose number of petals by clicking on image.
  4. Choose flower from the list.
  5. Read about flower, check other images.
  6. Go back, choose another one until you'll find right one.

Jumping around scenario:

You can jump between filter screens through left drawer where you can also see chosen values of attributes. When filter screen is loaded value of bounded attribute is deleted from the current filter. Counter in action bar will show you how many flowers fulfil chosen criteria. Once you have set all three attributes scrollable list of flowers appears. If counter shows less than 21 flowers you can jump right to the list by pressing counter without setting remaining attributes. Long press on counter will clear the filter.

Available actions:

  1. Press on counter display list of flowers. It is allowed only when counter value is less than 21.
  2. Long press on counter will clear the filter.
  3. Swipe from left or press on "hamburger" button display left drawer.
  4. Press on one of filter options in drawer will display correspondent filter screen. Value of bounded attribute is deleted from the filter.
  5. Press on Preferences option in drawer will display Preferences.
  6. Press on Help option in drawer will display Help (this screen).
  7. Press on About option in drawer will display About application screen.

Preferences:

  1. Preferred language: Choose preferred language.
  2. Use English when no text in preferred language exists: English texts will be loaded when no text in preferred language exists.
